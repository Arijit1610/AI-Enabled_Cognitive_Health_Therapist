ABOUT AI-HEALTH

The AI-Enabled Cognitive Health Therapist project is a research initiative that aims to develop an AI-powered chatbot that can provide cognitive behavioural therapy (CBT) to people with mental health conditions. One of its main advantages is its capacity to create customized CBT exercises and interventions, tailoring its recommendations to each user's unique needs and problems. The chatbot is being developed utilizing a range of methods, such as machine learning and natural language processing. Additionally, the chatbot continuously tracks users' progress over time and offers data-driven insights into their experience with mental health. Users and their healthcare professionals gain from this feedback loop, which helps them make wise choices on their way to better mental health

WHY AI-HEALTH

A critical step toward democratizing mental health care is being taken with the "AI-Enabled Cognitive Health Therapist" initiative. This ground-breaking approach delivers individualized and accessible therapy by utilizing artificial intelligence. It not only empowers consumers but also aligns with changing healthcare demands thanks to its easy user interface, ongoing customisation, and strict data privacy measures. It has the potential to alter mental health, lighten the load on conventional healthcare systems, and eventually improve quality of life for countless people around the world as it develops and adapts.
